## Key Wealth Assets

### Income Generation
- **Harvesters**: 33% chance for 1 FacCred
- **Postech Industry**: Variable income (loss to profit)
- **Venture Capital**: High risk, high reward
- **Pretech Manufactory**: Reliable high income

### Transport & Logistics
- **Freighter Contract**: 2-hex non-Force transport
- **Shipping Combine**: Mass 2-hex transport
- **Blockade Runners**: 3-hex military transport
- **Transit Web**: 3-hex Cunning/Wealth network

### Tech Advancement
- **Laboratory**: Makes world TL4
- **Pretech Researchers**: Makes world TL5 for C/W
- **R&D Department**: All worlds TL4 for Wealth

### Economic Warfare
- **Franchise**: Steals FacCreds
- **Monopoly**: Forces payments
- **Marketers**: Disables assets
- **Hostile Takeover**: Steals assets

### Special Abilities
- **Bank**: Ignores costs/losses
- **Medical Center**: Restores destroyed units
- **Surveyors**: Helps expansion
- **Local Investments**: Increases rival costs

## Wealth Asset Tactics

### Economic Engine
1. Build income-generating assets
2. Use Bank to protect from losses
3. Reinvest profits in expansion
4. Create monopolies for control

### Tech Development
1. Laboratory for basic tech
2. Pretech Researchers for advanced
3. R&D Department for widespread access
4. Build high-tech assets cheaply

### Market Manipulation
1. Marketers disable key assets
2. Hostile Takeover steals weakened targets
3. Franchise drains enemy resources
4. Commodities Broker reduces costs

### Logistics Network
1. Shipping Combine for mass movement
2. Transit Web for rapid deployment
3. Blockade Runners for military ops
4. Surveyors scout new territories

## Common Combinations

- Pretech Manufactory (income)

- Marketers (disruption)

### Expansion Fleet
- Surveyors (scouting)

## Income Management

### Reliable Income
- Harvesters: Low but steady
- Pretech Manufactory: High and consistent
- Monopoly: Force payments from rivals

### Variable Income
- Postech Industry: Can lose money
- Venture Capital: High risk/reward
- Franchise: Depends on successful attacks

### Cost Reduction
- Bank: Ignore one cost per turn
- Commodities Broker: Reduce purchase costs
- Medical Center: Cheaper repairs

## Maintenance Costs

Most Wealth assets have no maintenance, but:
- **Mercenaries**: 1 FacCred per turn
- **Pretech Researchers**: 1 FacCred per turn
- **Scavenger Fleet**: 2 FacCreds per turn

## Tech Level Manipulation

### Creating Access
- Laboratory: Basic TL4 access
- Pretech Researchers: Advanced TL5 for C/W
- R&D Department: Universal TL4 for Wealth

### Strategic Value
- Build advanced assets on primitive worlds
- Bypass local tech limitations
- Create competitive advantages

## Counter-Wealth Strategies

### Direct Attacks
- Target income-generating assets
- Destroy transport infrastructure
- Eliminate tech advantages

### Economic Pressure
- Force maintenance costs
- Block expansion attempts
- Steal or disable key assets

### Alternative Approaches
- Use Force to bypass Wealth defenses
- Cunning infiltration of facilities
- Diplomatic isolation
